# Dunham

a simple utility

we start with a ilst of word or phrase (string[])

- takes a folder of videos in any common format
- makes a list of words and time codes (enough to roughly detect where a word is by the 1/2 second) or any other way to achieve the goal
- create a new video which is a montage of all the times those words or phrases appear one after another

the first test case will be to scan videos for the word "Dunham" (different cases and soudalikes too)

for testing we download some youtube videos, see if we can get episodes of partial episodes of fringe (2008)
